Public version of Environmental Justice cases.

The dataset collects information from the webpage https://ejatlas.org/ and shares the location of the cases (as geometry in spatial datasets or Lat/Lon in csv) and their classification based on the mobilization result.

Cased were clustered using hierarchical cluster in 3 clusters: 
- reform cases (when mobilization result in some form of mediation and change)
- resists cases (when mobilization results in criminalization)
- transform cases (with mobilization resulting in some stopping of projects that result in environmental impacts)

Spatial datasets in 3 different foramts (.parquet, .gpkg, .shp and its companien files)
Dataframe in .csv
